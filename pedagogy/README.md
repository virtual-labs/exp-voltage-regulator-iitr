#### 3. Instructional Strategy
###### Name of Instructional Strategy  :    <u> Expositry
###### Assessment Method: Formative Assessment

<u> <b>Description: </b> Instructional Strategy will be implemented in the simulator as follows: </u>
<br>
This experiment contains the circuit of a voltage regulator using an op-amp to produce the output of 12 V.  Firstly, the student will make proper connections in the circuit to design the Voltage regulator. Student can also check/reset the connections. Then the students will vary the input voltage and analyse the regulated output voltage.
